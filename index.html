01 / CHARACTER ARCHITECTURE

Defining the Digital Identity

To build a recognizable brand without standard cameras, a stable face is mandatory. I trained a custom diffusion LoRA on a high-res dataset, hard-coding key facial proportions, hair density, and dramatic amber rim lighting into the weights.

02 / CONTINUITY CONTROL

Eliminating Frame Drift

AI generation is notoriously chaotic across multiple clips. For "Signal Zero," I mapped standard mesh grids onto the actor's face and used frame-guided seed structures inside Veo 3. The character remains coherent through heavy atmospheric filters and dramatic action cuts.

03 / DUAL INTERACTION

The Café Dialogues

For "Do You Know?", I cloned the digital avatar to create two interacting personalities. They hold deadpan debates on ethics and trolley problems, rendering realistic facial movements, speech pauses, and side-eye angles. No goofy hand gestures, just cold mechanical philosophy.

04 / COMPLETE AUTOMATION

Self-Running Asset

The end goal is absolute systemic freedom. The avatar works in tandem with an N8N scheduler, rendering and publishing overnight. It handles the writing, generation, lipsyncing, and uploading without a single human keystroke. A content creator that doesn't sleep.
